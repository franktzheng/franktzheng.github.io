Symplectic Topology Student Seminar (Fall 2024)

Topic: Symplectic topology of cotangent bundles

Organizers: Shuhao Li and Frank Zheng

Description: Given a closed smooth manifold \(Q\), does the symplectic topology on \(T^*Q\) determine the smooth topology of \(Q\)? The attempt to answer this question leads to the study of exact embedded Lagrangians in \(T^*Q\) (“nearby Lagrangians”), culminating in the striking result by Abouzaid-Kragh that nearby Lagrangians share the same simple homotopy type as the base manifold. These results followed from (and lead to) developments of categorical symplectic geometry, which also has applications in various other problems.
